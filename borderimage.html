border-image-repeat: stretch

STRETCH - The border image is stretched to fill the area.

border-image-repeat: repeat

REPEAT - The border image is tiled; may get clipped if not fitting exactly.

border-image-repeat: round

ROUND - The image is tiled and scaled to fit without clipping.

border-image-repeat: space

SPACE - The image is tiled and space is evenly distributed between tiles.
